## 🎯 Common Tasks

### Add a Member
1. Go to **Members** page
2. Click **+ Add Member**
3. Enter: Name, ID, Phone
4. Click **Add Member**

### Assign a Role
1. Find member in Members table
2. Click **Shield icon**
3. Select role (Secretary, Chairperson, etc.)
4. Click **Assign Role**

### Manage Beneficiaries
1. Find member in Members table
2. Click **Users icon**
3. Click **Add Beneficiary** tab
4. Enter details
5. Click **Add Beneficiary**

### Create Meeting Minutes (Secretary)
1. Login as Secretary
2. Go to **Meeting Minutes**
3. Click **+ New Minutes**
4. Fill in details
5. Click **Save**
